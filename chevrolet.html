Chevrolet India



Chevrolet India dealership was Ambala Automobile's first venture established in 2011. It wass the largest selling dealer in North India in Sales, Service and Spares and had its own world class showroom and state of the art workshop and bodyshop under one roof in Ambala (Haryana).

Being the part of Chevrolet we achieved many awards and recognitions-

• Achieved First position in Chevrolet U First Camp organized in 2017 with respect to all parameters.
• Highest spare part sale in complete North Region in 2016.
• The Only dealership in complete North Region to achieve Chevrolet Premium League in 2016.
• Achieved First position in Skill Development Contest of service advisors in 2016 at all India Level.
• All INDIA winner to achieve the Excellent Sales Target Achievement Award in Dec 2015.
• Achieved the Excellent Sales Target Achievement Award in Dec 2011/12/13/14.
• Consistent achiever of Big Finish Programme held by CSIPL since 2012.
• Only dealer in HARYANA to achieve the Excellent Sales Target Achievement Award in Dec 2010.


We have recently started our Chandigarh Service Operation branch in 2017 and have achieved a healthy figure of 1000+ service vehicles per month in a very short span of time. Our company is committed to offering world class customer service that builds lifetime customer relationships.
