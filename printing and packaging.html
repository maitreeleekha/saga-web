Quality Policy

Quality is the foundation of our company and is fully embedded in our purpose and values. SAGA is based upon our quality image and our reputation for consistently delivering high-quality products. At SAGA, we are involved in and dedicated to achieving high Quality standards for our products and services through the application of the SAGA Management System (SMS) for Quality & SHE (Safety, Health & Environment). Fully aligned to ISO standards, SMS for Quality & SHE is implemented across our organization and verified by independent third party certification bodies.

1. Guarantees product safety and full compliance by respecting our policies, principles and standards with full transparency.
2. Conscious efforts to improve the product quality to consistently cater packaging needs by utilizing slate of art manufacturing facilities and ensuring the delivery as per the customer expectation.
3. Developing and maintaining open and construction communication with our customers, suppliers and employees.
4. Developing and giving training to our employees so that they can be fully involved in and head towards achieving this policy.
