About Us


SAGA GROUP came into existence in 2011 and its Head Office is located in Yamuna Nagar (Haryana). SAGA heads the Ambala Autombile and Shaktiman Bio Agro Pvt. Ltd.
We welcome the opportunity to continue our growing relationships with each of our customers and look forward to develop relationship with new customers. With SAGA, you get our experience in superior production quality, excellent customer service, and reliability that has made us a leader in our sector.

Ambala Autombile

Ambala Automobile India Ltd. was established in 2011 and presently holds dealerships in Mahindra Automotive Division, Mahindra Trucks and Buses and Chevrolet Service Plus. The outlets and service stations are situated in Kurukshetra, Yamuna Nagar, Ambala and Rajpura.
We are a team of dedicated professionals who work hard to maintain the sales which can be proved with the sales record of our business for the past years. Our company is committed to offering world class customer service that builds lifetime customer relationships.

Shaktiman Bio Agro Pvt. Ltd.

Shaktiman Bio Agro Industries Pvt Ltd. was formed in 2013 as A Printing and Packaging Unit. We are sovereign manufacturers of premium quality corrugated boxes and cardboard cartons in Bulk Quantities that assist you in carrying your valuable belongings to any part of the world – safe and secure!
We are a dynamic organization understanding the needs of the changing business scenarios and hence we keep on upgrading our technology and encompassing automation has been our constant endeavour.
We cater to various industries worldwide including FMCG, Industrial Goods, Food & Beverages, Pharmaceuticals, Apparel Industry, Electronics etc. We can give your brand a new identity by providing you with innovative designing and appealing shapes and sizes.



Khanna Paper

In 2018, SAGA commenced the trading of KHANNA PAPER. Khanna Paper Mills has emerged as a leader of the Indian Paper Industry by promoting the recycling of waste paper as the feedstock. Khanna Paper Mills Private Limited, is an ISO 9001:2000 certified company, manufacturing quality paper and board by recycling of waste paper as the feedstock for domestic consumption for a little over 4 decades. Although paper is traditionally identified with reading and writing, communication has now been replaced by packaging as the single largest category of paper use at 41% of all paper used.
